Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, simply follow these easy steps. First, click on the 'Sign Up' button located at the top right corner of the page. You will be directed to the registration form where you'll need to provide some basic information such as your name, email address, and a secure password. Once you've filled out the required details, click the 'Create Account' button. A confirmation email will be sent to the provided email address. Click on the confirmation link within the email to verify your account, and you're all set! You can now log in using your email and password, and start enjoying all the benefits of our platform.

If you've lost or forgotten your password, don't worry; we've got you covered. Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page, and you'll be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account. After you submit your email address, we'll send you a password reset link to that email. Simply check your inbox (and your spam folder, just in case), and click on the reset link. You'll be directed to a page where you can create a new password for your account. Once you've set a new password, you'll be able to log in with your updated credentials. It's that easy! If you encounter any issues during this process, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
